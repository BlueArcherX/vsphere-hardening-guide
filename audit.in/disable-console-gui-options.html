Audit Check disable-console-gui-options

Denial of Cut and Paste operations from within the vCenter or vSphere client console prevents exfiltration or infiltration of data by that avenue

The affected VMX settings are: isolation.tools.setGUIOptions.enable=TRUE

Audit Check: 


    vmrun -T esx -h https://<host ip>/sdk -u <userid> -p <password> readvariable <path to vmx file> isolation.tools.setGUIOptions.enable
    (note that this can only check running VMs, use vmware-cmd for dormant hosts)

In PowerCLI:
    $vm | Get-AdvancedSetting -Name  "isolation.tools.setGUIOptions.enable"

A returned value of "TRUE" or blank indicates Compliance, "False" indicates non-compliance
