Audit Check label-portgroups

Naming VLANs and Portgroups with meaningful names is an important step in creating an infrastructure. It makes troubleshooting much simpler, and makes the infrastructure to some extent “self documenting” and easier to administer. It is generally desired that VLAN names within a virtual infrastructure should follow the same naming convention as in the physical environment, and in fact should be identical for matching VLANs.

Audit Check:
	vicfg-vswitch.pl --server <server ip address> --username <username> --password <password> -l

or
	esxcli <connection information> network vswitch standard list
or
        Get-VirtualPortGroup
