Audit Check label-vswitches

Similar to naming VLANs, naming vSwitches with meaningful names is an important step in creating an infrastructure. It makes troubleshooting much simpler, and makes the infrastructure to some extent “self documenting” and easier to administer. Where possible, the naming of virtual switches should match the same naming convention as is established for switches in the physical infrastructure.

Audit Check:
	vicfg-vswitch.pl --server <server ip address> --username <username> --password <password> -l
or
	esxcli <connection information> network vswitch standard list
or
        Get-VirtualSwitch
